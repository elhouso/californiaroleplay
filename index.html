Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

To join California Roleplay, you need to join our Discord server first and follow the instructions provided there.

What roles are available?

We offer various roles including police officers, firefighters, medics, civilians, and more.

Do I need to apply for departments?

Yes, you need to submit an application for departments like Police and Fire. Check our Discord for application details.

Are there age requirements?

Our server is open to all ages, but we maintain a mature and respectful environment.
