Server Rules

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a great experience for everyone

  • Respect All Players

    Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying will not be tolerated.

  • Realistic Roleplay

    Maintain realism in all scenarios. Avoid unrealistic actions that would break immersion.

  • No Metagaming

    Do not use information your character wouldn't know in-game. Keep roleplay and real-life separate.

  • Follow Chain of Command

    Respect department hierarchies and follow orders from superior officers.

  • Proper Communications

    Use appropriate radio codes and maintain professional communication during roleplay.

  • No Combat Logging

    Leaving the game to avoid roleplay consequences is prohibited.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our server

How do I join the server?

To join our server, you'll need to join our Discord server first. There, you'll find the link to our Roblox group and server. Follow the instructions in the #getting-started channel to complete the application process.

How do I apply for a department?

Department applications are available on our Discord server. You'll need to complete the application form and attend an interview with department staff. Successful applicants will receive training before being accepted.

What are the age requirements?

Players must be at least 13 years old to join our community. Some department positions may have higher age requirements due to maturity needs.

Are there scheduled server events?

Yes! We host regular events including patrol operations, training sessions, disaster scenarios, and community gatherings. Check the events calendar on our Discord for upcoming activities.

Can I transfer from another ER:LC server?

We consider transfers from other servers on a case-by-case basis. You'll need to provide references and service history. Contact an admin through Discord for more information.
