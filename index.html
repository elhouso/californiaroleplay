Server Rules 📜

🛑 General Conduct

Be respectful to all players regardless of role or rank. Harassment, discrimination, and offensive language will not be tolerated. Remember this is a community-focused server aimed at providing fun for everyone.

🚓 Roleplay Guidelines

Maintain realistic roleplay at all times. Follow proper radio codes, adhere to department protocols, and respect chain of command. No breaking character or "power gaming" during active scenarios.

🚦 Traffic Stops

All traffic stops must be conducted with valid reasons. Use proper procedure including announcing location, vehicle description, and plate number. Wait for dispatch acknowledgment before proceeding.

🔫 Use of Force

Follow realistic escalation of force protocols. Verbal commands come first, followed by non-lethal options when necessary. Lethal force should only be used when life is threatened.

🚑 EMS Protocol

EMS personnel must respond to all medical emergencies promptly. Proper triage procedures must be followed. Coordinate with law enforcement when entering potentially dangerous scenes.

🔥 Fire Department

Respond to fire calls with appropriate equipment. Follow proper protocols for different types of fires. Coordinate with EMS for any injuries resulting from fire incidents.

⚠️ Violations

Rule violations will result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity and frequency. All staff decisions are final, though appeals may be submitted through proper channels.

Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

How do I join the server?
+

To join our California Roleplay server, first join our Discord community where you'll find the server link and instructions. You'll need to own Emergency Response: Liberty County on Roblox. After joining our Discord, you'll need to complete a brief application and orientation process.

How do I apply for a department?
+

Department applications are available on our Discord server. Each department has specific requirements and an application process. After submitting your application, you'll be contacted for an interview and training if accepted.

What makes this server different from other ER:LC servers?
+

Our server stands out with its authentic California-themed experience, including custom vehicles, uniforms, and locations inspired by real California agencies. We have a dedicated staff team ensuring high-quality roleplay and an active community with regular events and scenarios.

Are there scheduled events?
+

Yes, we host regular scheduled events including patrol operations, major incident response exercises, community meetings, and special holiday-themed scenarios. Check our Discord server for the updated event calendar.

Do I need any special equipment or mods?
+

No special mods are required as we operate within the standard ER:LC game. However, we recommend having a working microphone for better communication. We also use Discord for off-game communication and coordination.
