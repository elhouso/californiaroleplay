About California Roleplay

Learn about our server's mission and the team behind the experience.

California Roleplay was founded in 2023 with a mission to create the most immersive and enjoyable roleplay experience possible in Emergency Response: Liberty County. Our server is built on the principles of realistic roleplay, community engagement, and continuous improvement.

Our team consists of experienced roleplayers, developers, and community managers who are passionate about creating an environment where everyone can enjoy their time and create memorable experiences.

From humble beginnings, we've grown to become one of the most popular roleplay servers in the game, with hundreds of active members and a reputation for quality and innovation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our server and community.

How do I join California Roleplay?

To join California Roleplay, you need to join our Discord server first, complete a simple application process, and then you'll receive instructions on how to join our game server in Emergency Response: Liberty County.

Are there age requirements?

Yes, we require all members to be at least 13 years old to join our community, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service.

What roles can I play on the server?

Our server offers a wide range of roles including law enforcement (police, sheriff, highway patrol), emergency services (fire, EMS), civilian jobs, and criminal organizations. Each role has its own unique progression system and benefits.

Do I need to own Emergency Response: Liberty County?

Yes, you'll need to own the game Emergency Response: Liberty County on Roblox to play on our server. The game itself has a small purchase price on the Roblox platform.

How are rules enforced on the server?

We have a dedicated team of moderators and administrators who monitor the server and ensure rules are being followed. We use a strike system for rule violations, with consequences ranging from warnings to temporary or permanent bans depending on the severity and frequency of violations.
