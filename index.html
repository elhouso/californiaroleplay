Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

To join our server, first join our Discord server using the link provided. Then follow the instructions in the #getting-started channel to apply for whitelist and join the game.

How do I apply for a department?

Department applications are available in our Discord server. Navigate to the #department-applications channel and follow the instructions to apply for your preferred department.

Are there age requirements?

Yes, we require all members to be at least 14 years old to join our community. Some departments may have higher age requirements.

What are the server rules?

Our server has a comprehensive set of rules to ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone. You can view the complete rules on our GitBook page, accessible via the "View Rules" button on our homepage.

How can I report a player?

If you need to report a player for breaking the rules, please use the #report-a-player channel in our Discord server. Make sure to provide evidence such as screenshots or video recordings when applicable.
