Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Join our Discord server using the link provided, read our rules, and follow the application process detailed in the welcome channels.

What departments can I join?

We offer Police Department, Fire Department, EMS, Civilian roles, and more specialized divisions within each department.

Do I need to apply for departments?

Yes, most departments require an application and training process. Details can be found in our Discord server.

What are the server rules?

Our comprehensive rules are available on our GitBook page. Please review them before joining to ensure a positive experience.

How often are events hosted?

We host multiple events every week, including department-specific training, community-wide scenarios, and special holiday events.

Is there an age requirement?

We require members to be at least 13 years old, with certain leadership positions reserved for members 16+.

How can I become staff?

Staff applications open periodically based on server needs. Active, positive community members with a good record are considered.

Are there rank-ups in departments?

Yes, all departments have rank structures. Members can advance through active participation, following protocols, and passing rank exams.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We have a dedicated suggestions channel in our Discord where members can propose new ideas for the server.

How do I report an issue?

You can report issues through our ticket system in Discord. Staff members respond promptly to all reports and concerns.
