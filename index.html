About California Roleplay

The story behind our server and the team that makes it possible.

California Roleplay was established in 2023 with one goal: to create the most authentic roleplay experience in Emergency Response: Liberty County. What began as a small community of passionate roleplayers has grown into one of the most respected servers in the game.

Our team consists of experienced gamers, developers, and community managers who are dedicated to creating an environment where every player can thrive. We believe in the power of immersive roleplay to create memorable experiences and lasting friendships.

With thousands of active members and countless stories created, we continue to evolve and improve, setting the standard for what a roleplay server should be.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining and playing on our server.

How do I join California Roleplay?

To join our server, you'll need to join our Discord first, complete a brief application, and then follow the instructions to join our private server in Emergency Response: Liberty County.

Is there an age requirement?

Yes, all members must be at least 13 years old to join our community, in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service and to maintain a mature roleplay environment.

What careers can I choose?

We offer multiple career paths including law enforcement (police, sheriff, highway patrol), emergency services (fire, EMS), civilian roles (taxi driver, mechanic, store owner), and criminal organizations. Each path has its own unique progression system.

Do I need to own the game?

Yes, you'll need to own Emergency Response: Liberty County on Roblox to play on our server. The game itself costs a small amount of Robux on the Roblox platform.

How are rules enforced?

We have a dedicated team of moderators who ensure rules are followed. We use a progressive discipline system, with consequences ranging from warnings to permanent bans depending on the severity of violations.
