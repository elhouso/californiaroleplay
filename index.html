Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join California Roleplay?

To join California Roleplay, simply join our Discord server using the button above, read our rules on GitBook, and follow the application process detailed in our Discord server.

What roles are available?

We offer a variety of roles including law enforcement (police and sheriff), fire department, emergency medical services, and civilian roles including various jobs and businesses.

Do I need to own Emergency Response: Liberty County?

Yes, you need to own the Roblox game "Emergency Response: Liberty County" to participate in our roleplay server.

Are there age requirements?

We welcome players of all ages, but we expect all members to behave maturely and follow our community guidelines and rules.

How often do you host events?

We host regular events including training sessions, community gatherings, competitions, and special roleplay scenarios. Check our Discord for the event schedule.
