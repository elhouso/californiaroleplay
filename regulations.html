Regulations

Discord Rules

(1.0) Treat all community members with the utmost respect and refrain from any form of bullying, harassment, or threats at all times.

(1.2) Your Discord nickname should match your ROBLOX username, with the exception of using callsigns, which should be separated by a pipe symbol.

(1.3) We have a strict zero-tolerance policy for posting or sharing any NSFW or hateful content, including text, images, voice chats, or links.

(1.4) Please respect the content filter and avoid attempting to bypass it. Additionally, use profanity sparingly, and note that racial slurs are strictly prohibited.

(1.5) Make sure to use channels for their intended purposes and engage appropriately.

(1.6) Drama or disrespect toward other servers is not allowed. Any related discussions should be taken to direct messages.

(1.7) For clarity and consistency, English is the only language permitted within the community.

(1.8) For assistance, please avoid pinging supervisors or higher staff directly. Instead, create a support ticket. Please also refrain from pinging HR members.
