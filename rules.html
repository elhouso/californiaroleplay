Server Rules

Our Guidelines

By joining our server, you agree to follow all the rules listed below. Failure to comply may result in warnings, kicks, or permanent bans from California Roleplay.

General Rules

  • Respect All Players: Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will be tolerated.
  • No Exploiting: Using glitches, hacks, or any unfair advantages is strictly prohibited.
  • No RDM (Random Death Match): Do not kill players without valid roleplay reason.
  • No VDM (Vehicle Death Match): Do not use vehicles as weapons to kill other players.
  • No Metagaming: Using information your character wouldn't know in-game is prohibited.
  • No Powergaming: Forcing scenarios on others or acting in unrealistic ways is not allowed.
  • Stay In Character: Maintain roleplay at all times in main chat. Use Discord for OOC (Out of Character) communication.
  • Listen to Staff: Staff decisions are final. If you have an issue, take it to Discord after the session.

Law Enforcement Rules

  • Follow Chain of Command: Respect your superiors and follow the department's hierarchy.
  • Use Proper Procedures: Follow realistic police procedures during traffic stops, arrests, and investigations.
  • Radio Communication: Maintain professional radio communication using proper codes and callsigns.
  • Weapon Usage: Only use lethal force when absolutely necessary and follow escalation of force procedures.
  • Uniform Standards: Wear only approved uniforms and vehicle liveries.
  • Jurisdiction: Respect department jurisdictions and boundaries.
  • No Corruption: Law enforcement officers may not engage in corrupt activities.

Fire/EMS Rules

  • Priority Response: Always respond to emergency calls promptly and prioritize based on severity.
  • Medical Realism: Provide realistic medical care based on injuries.
  • Equipment Usage: Use appropriate equipment for different scenarios.
  • Scene Safety: Ensure scene safety before treating patients.
  • Hospital Procedures: Follow proper hospital check-in procedures.
  • Patient Confidentiality: Maintain confidentiality of patient information.

Civilian Rules

  • Traffic Laws: Follow basic traffic laws unless involved in an active roleplay scenario.
  • Realistic Roleplay: Maintain realistic behavior and scenarios.
  • Criminal Activity: Criminal roleplay is allowed but must be realistic and well-planned.
  • No Server Hopping: Do not leave the server to avoid consequences or arrests.
  • Robberies: Robberies must be planned and executed with realism. No constant robbery spam.
  • Character Consistency: Maintain consistent character traits and background.
  • No Mass Shootings: Terrorist attacks or mass shootings are strictly prohibited.

Communication Rules

  • Radio Discipline: Keep radio communications clear and relevant.
  • No Mic Spam: Excessive talking, music, or disruptive noises on voice channels is prohibited.
  • Age-Appropriate Language: Keep language clean and appropriate.
  • No Toxicity: Toxic behavior or language will not be tolerated.
  • Proper Callouts: Use appropriate callouts for locations and situations.
  • Emergency Channels: Emergency radio channels are for emergency use only.

Consequences

  • First Offense: Verbal warning or temporary timeout.
  • Second Offense: Formal warning and possible temporary ban (24-48 hours).
  • Third Offense: 1-week ban from the server.
  • Serious Violations: Immediate permanent ban for severe rule violations.
  • Appeals: Ban appeals can be submitted via our Discord after a 72-hour cooling period.

These rules are subject to change. It is your responsibility to stay updated with the latest rules posted on our Discord server.
